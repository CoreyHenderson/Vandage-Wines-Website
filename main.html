Wine Questions

What is your range of wines?
Our Reds:
- Cabonet Sauvignon  
- Merlot  
- Shiraz  
- Sweet Red  
Our Whites:
- Chardonnay  
- Sauvignon Blanc  
- Pinot Grigio  
- White Merlot  

How old is your vintage?
Some red wines we tend to grow to about ten years like the shiraz, and others like the merlot go to about 5 years. Our White's sit between 5 and 7 years old.
A perfect age for when they are at their prime flavours.

What is your recommended best wine you offer?
Our most preserved and prized vintage is the 7 year old Sauvignon Blanc

How do you age your vintage?
We age our wine in bottles that are stored in a cellar on shelves.
We keep the cellar at a relatively cool environment in order for the wine to
develop complexity and more aromatic bouquet.

Do you have a wine shop?
Yes, we have our wine store located next to the fresh grape vinyards in east Melbourne, Australia. Located
in Burwood, our wine vinyard store is open 7 days a week 10am - 5pm. Inside you'll find displays of our
most prized wines along with a taste testing table with choice of anything from ours selections.

Payment Questions

Is Ordering Online Secure?
Yes. We take the utmost care with the information that you provide us when placing an order on our website
(or through any other means). The server that hosts our bookstore encrypts the transmission of all credit
card and personal customer information using the Internet-standard SSL (Secure Sockets Layer) protocol.
During the encryption process, information is scrambled into small bits of code that cannot be read as they
travel to us over the Internet. Once we receive the encrypted information, we use a private, one-of-a-kind key to decode it.

How do I know that you received my order? Will I get a recipt?
The moment you proceed with the payment our servers will send an automated email to the one your provide,
showing confirmation on purchase along with an attached recipt for the purchased product(s).

What are accepted payment options?
All payment methods include:
- Mastercard/Visa/AMEX
- PayPal (secure payment service)
-Bank Transfer (2 day process)

How do I view my shopping cart
A confirmation of the items you have chosen to purchase will be displayed for you before you finalise your
payment. A recipt of the items will also be emailed to you

Postal Questions

How long will it take for my order to arrive?
Standard Australia Postage is 5-7 business days. Express Postage is 1-3 days within Australia. If you are
an overseas customer, delivery takes approimately 10-14 days

What if I need to change my address or suspend delivery of my order?
If you have passed the payment process and wish to change the address of delivery, go ahead and contact us
by phone or email, and we'll gladly rearrange the details for you. Just make sure to provide you're name and
recipt number so we know which order to change

What is your returns policy?
We want you to be completely satisfied with your order. If for some reason you are not and you wish to
return your purchase, you’ll need to contact us via email or phone from the contact us page. Once you
receive postage address and an approval number, post the item(s) sealed in a secured safe container. When
we recieve you're item(s) we will resend you a more fresh one or another of your choosing

What if I haven't recieved my order yet and it has exceeded the specified postage time?
Once we have recieved an online order, it is approved and posted by our team as soon as possible.
When it is posted we send an email/SMS containing the postage tracking number. Using this number, you
should be able to track the location of your item
